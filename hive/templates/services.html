Our Strategic plan

Aims to develop an innovation business network that will bridge the worlds of enterprises and governments to stimulate innovation, & enrich the experience of startups in Rwanda.

Combine


Combine open innovation learning & action research that is relevant to the needs of enterprises and governments by stimulating entrepreneurial spirit and provide a conducive working environment to the participants to nurture their innovative ideas.

Embed


Embed technologies and innovation to produce successful and financially viable firms that can survive on their own to help create and grow young businesses by providing them with necessary support and financial and technical services.

Contribute


Contribute to the growth and success of emerging technology businesses and to foster an entrepreneurial culture, by providing the participants opportunities to transform their business ideas to reality and improve skills in IT in order to be Developed quickly.

Coordinate


Coordinate and Liaise with private and public sector funding sources to increase the successful development of new businesses, job creation and employment in specific sectors that are aligned with country’s unique areas of opportunity.
